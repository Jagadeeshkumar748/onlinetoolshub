Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the website OnlineToolsHub.in.

Information collection

OnlineToolsHub.in does not require you to create an account or provide personal details in order to use the tools. Some basic, non-personal information may be collected automatically, such as your browser type, device type, approximate location and pages visited. This is standard web analytics data and is used only to understand and improve site usage.

Cookies & third-party services

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic analytics and, in the future, for displaying third-party advertisements (e.g., Google AdSense). These third parties may use their own cookies to show relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

Uploaded data

For tools that allow you to enter or upload data (for example, calculators, date inputs, or future file tools), the calculations are performed in your browser using JavaScript. The site does not intentionally store your input values on the server, except for limited and temporary technical logs required to keep the service running securely.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time as new tools are added or as legal requirements change. The revised policy will always be available on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the details on the Contact page.
