What is a Work Hour Calculator?

A work hour calculator helps employees, freelancers and employers track working hours accurately. It calculates the total hours worked after deducting break time, identifies overtime hours (any hours exceeding 8 hours in a day), and computes regular pay, overtime pay (at 1.5x the hourly rate) and total pay. This is especially useful for hourly workers, part-time employees and freelancers who need to track billable hours.

How to Use This Calculator

Enter the start time, end time and break duration for each working day. You can add multiple rows for different days of the week using the "Add Row" button. Optionally enter your hourly rate to see pay calculations. Click "Calculate Hours & Pay" to see the daily breakdown with regular and overtime hours, along with weekly totals. Pay is displayed in Indian number format.

Formula

Work Hours = End Time - Start Time - Break Duration

Regular Hours = min(Work Hours, 8)

Overtime Hours = max(Work Hours - 8, 0)

Regular Pay = Regular Hours x Hourly Rate

Overtime Pay = Overtime Hours x Hourly Rate x 1.5

Total Pay = Regular Pay + Overtime Pay

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as overtime?

In this calculator, any hours worked beyond 8 hours in a single day are counted as overtime. Overtime is typically paid at 1.5 times the regular hourly rate, which is the standard overtime multiplier in many regions.

Can I calculate for a full week?

Yes. Click "Add Row" to add entries for each day of the week. The calculator will show a daily breakdown and compute weekly totals for hours, regular pay, overtime pay and total pay.

Is the hourly rate required?

No. The hourly rate is optional. If you only need to track hours worked and overtime, you can leave the hourly rate field empty. Pay calculations will only appear when a rate is provided.
