Salary Hike Calculator

Calculate your new salary after a hike or increment. See monthly and annual breakdown instantly.

What is a Salary Hike?

A salary hike (also called an increment or raise) is an increase in an employee's compensation, usually expressed as a percentage of their current salary. Salary hikes are typically offered during annual appraisals, promotions, or when switching jobs. Understanding the exact impact of a hike percentage on your take-home pay is crucial for financial planning and salary negotiations.

How to Use This Calculator

Select whether your salary is monthly or annual. Enter your current salary amount and the hike percentage offered. Click "Calculate New Salary" to instantly see your new salary, the hike amount, and a complete monthly and annual breakdown. This helps you understand the real impact of a hike on your earnings.

Salary Hike Formula

The formula used by this calculator:

Hike Amount = Current Salary x Hike% / 100

New Salary = Current Salary + Hike Amount

For example, if your current monthly salary is ₹50,000 and you receive a 20% hike, your hike amount is ₹10,000 and new salary is ₹60,000 per month.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good salary hike percentage in India?

In India, a typical annual hike ranges from 8% to 15% during appraisals. When switching jobs, a hike of 20% to 40% is common. High performers in IT and tech sectors may receive hikes of 25% or more during appraisals.

How do I calculate salary hike percentage?

Hike Percentage = ((New Salary - Old Salary) / Old Salary) x 100. For example, if your salary increased from ₹50,000 to ₹60,000, your hike percentage is ((60000 - 50000) / 50000) x 100 = 20%.

Does this calculator account for taxes?

This calculator shows the gross salary before taxes. Your actual in-hand salary will be lower after deductions for income tax, provident fund, professional tax, and other statutory deductions. Use our Salary Calculator for a detailed CTC to in-hand breakdown.
