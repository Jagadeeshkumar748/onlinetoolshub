Age Difference Calculator

Calculate the exact age difference between two people in years, months and days.

What is an Age Difference Calculator?

An age difference calculator is a tool that computes the exact time gap between two people based on their dates of birth. It shows the difference in years, months and days, determines who is older, and provides interesting statistics about the age gap. This is useful for couples, family members, friends or anyone curious about the precise age difference between two individuals.

How to Use This Calculator

Enter the date of birth for both people. Optionally, enter their names for personalised results. Click "Calculate Age Difference" to see the exact difference in years, months and days, along with each person's current age and fun facts about the age gap. The calculator automatically determines who is older and shows the results accordingly.

Formula

The age difference is calculated by finding the absolute difference between the two dates of birth. The result is expressed in complete years, remaining months and remaining days. Each person's current age is computed from their date of birth to today's date.

Age Difference = |DOB1 - DOB2|

Current Age = Today - DOB

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this calculator account for leap years?

Yes. The calculator uses JavaScript's built-in Date object which correctly handles leap years. If someone was born on 29 February, the calculation adjusts for years where this date does not exist.

Can I compare ages of people born in different centuries?

Yes. You can enter any valid date of birth. The calculator works accurately for dates across different centuries and even different millennia.

What do the fun facts show?

The fun facts section shows the total age difference in different units such as total days, total weeks, and the percentage by which the older person's age exceeds the younger person's age.
