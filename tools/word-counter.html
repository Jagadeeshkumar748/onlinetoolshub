What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is an online tool that instantly counts the number of words, characters, lines, and paragraphs in any text you type or paste. It is widely used by students writing essays with word limits, content creators optimizing for social media character limits, and SEO professionals tracking content length for search engine guidelines. Unlike a simple character count, this tool gives you a complete breakdown of your text statistics in real time.

How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text into the text area above.
  2. View your live word count, character count (with and without spaces), line count, and paragraph count in the statistics panel.
  3. Use the "Copy text" button to copy your text to the clipboard, or "Clear" to start fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between word count and character count?

Word count measures the total number of words separated by spaces, while character count measures every individual letter, number, symbol, and space. For example, "Hello World" has 2 words but 11 characters (including the space).

Is this tool accurate for all languages?

This word counter works well for languages that use spaces to separate words, such as English, French, Spanish, and Hindi (Romanized). For languages like Chinese or Japanese that do not use spaces between words, the character count will still be accurate.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
